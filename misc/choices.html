Technology Choices

Choices available when adopting VR in STEM:

Firstly, educators need to evaluate the range and quality of STEM-focused VR content and applications. Not all VR experiences are equally effective for teaching STEM concepts,
so it is crucial to select those that align with curriculum goals and offer meaningful learning experiences. Secondly, considering the compatibility of VR systems is vital. Different
VR devices may have varying technical specifications and software requirements, which need to be assessed to ensure seamless integration with existing STEM resources and infrastructure.
By critically assessing these choices, educators can make informed decisions to maximize the benefits of VR technology in enhancing STEM education.
